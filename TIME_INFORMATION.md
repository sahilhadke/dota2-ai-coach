# Dota 2 Time-Dependent Mechanics and Spawn Intervals

## 1. Runes
* **Bounty Runes:** Spawn at **0:00** and respawn every **3 minutes** (3:00, 6:00, 9:00, etc.) in the primary jungle locations.
* **Water Runes:** Spawn at **2:00** and **4:00** only, appearing at both river locations simultaneously.
* **Power Runes:** Spawn at **6:00** and respawn every **2 minutes** (8:00, 10:00, 12:00, etc.) at one of the two river locations randomly.
* **Wisdom Runes:** Spawn at **7:00** and respawn every **7 minutes** (14:00, 21:00, 28:00, etc.) at the map edges near the offlane Tier 2 towers.

## 2. Map Objectives
* **Lotus Pools:** Spawn 1 Healing Lotus every **3 minutes** (3:00, 6:00, 9:00, etc.). Each pool can hold a maximum of 6 lotuses.
* **Tormentors:** Spawn at exactly **20:00**. Once killed, a Tormentor has a strict **10-minute** respawn timer from the exact moment of its death.
* **Day/Night Cycle:** The cycle shifts every **5 minutes**. 
    * Daytime begins at 0:00, 10:00, 20:00, etc.
    * Nighttime begins at 5:00, 15:00, 25:00, etc.
* **Roshan Map Movement:** Tied directly to the Day/Night cycle. Roshan runs to the Radiant pit during the Day and the Dire pit during the Night. 
* **Roshan Respawn Window:** When killed, Roshan respawns at a random time between **8 to 11 minutes** after his death. 
* **Aegis of the Immortal:** Expires exactly **5 minutes** after a player picks it up.
* **Watchers:** If an enemy destroys a captured Watcher, it automatically respawns exactly **2 minutes** later.

## 3. Creeps
* **Lane Creeps:** Spawn at **0:00** and every **30 seconds** thereafter (at the x:00 and x:30 marks of every minute).
* **Siege Creeps (Catapults):** Begin spawning at **5:00** and appear every **5 minutes** (10:00, 15:00, 20:00, etc.) alongside the regular creep wave.
* **Neutral Creeps:** Initial spawn is at **1:00**. They respawn at the **0-second mark of every minute** (2:00, 3:00, 4:00, etc.), but only if the camp's invisible spawn box is completely empty of units and wards.

## 4. Neutral Item Tier Drops
Neutral items become available to drop from jungle creeps at specific time thresholds:
* **Tier 1:** Minute **7:00**
* **Tier 2:** Minute **17:00**
* **Tier 3:** Minute **27:00**
* **Tier 4:** Minute **37:00**
* **Tier 5:** Minute **60:00**

## 5. Wards and Courier
* **Observer Wards:** Restock in the shop every **135 seconds**. Once placed, they last for **6 minutes**.
* **Sentry Wards:** Restock in the shop every **70 seconds**. Once placed, they last for **7 minutes**.
* **Flying Courier Upgrade:** Couriers automatically gain flying movement at minute **4:00**.
